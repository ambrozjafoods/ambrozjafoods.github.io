Cheese and Potato Pierogi

Sizeable doughy pockets filled with cheddar cheese and potato. Fry them on butter and medium heat so they're lightly crispy on both sides!

Ingredients: (dough) unbleached flour, milk, water, eggs, sea salt, canola oil (filling) potatoes, cheddar-style cheese, butter, onions, sour cream, vegetable seasoning, black pepper
